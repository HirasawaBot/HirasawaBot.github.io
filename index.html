What is Hirasawa?

Hirasawa is a Discord bot I am creating for fun. It currently just has random commands I feel like making. If you have a suggestion for a command, I would love to hear it!

What can Hirasawa do?

At the moment, not alot. She can currently fetch osu! profiles and statistics. I am taking suggestions, which you can give using the suggest command in Discord. Thanks for the support!
